Factory History and Management Sourcing and Quality Control Attention to Details(Management and Shop Floor)

30 years of establishment has strengthened our sourcing capacity and relationships with famous tanneries in France, Italy, and Spain, as well as reliable domestic tanneries in China. At the pre-production stage, technicians are sent to tanneries to conduct pre-production quality control to ensure the product has a right start.

Following our pre-production QC at our suppliers’ sites, each skin is further checked once it has been distributed to our cutters. Pre-cutting color selection is essential in minimizing color deviation within a garment or a handbag.

Quality Control continues as garments or handbags are cut. A color-checking process is in place to double-check for color difference and skin defects before pieces are transported to the sewing lines.

Once at the sewing lines, a production manager circulates among the sewing lines to monitor the completion of a garment, handbag, or belt at each step. Such constant supervision and communication among sewing group leaders ensure consistency and perfection.
